FlowKeep

Privacy Policy

Effective February 12, 2026 · Last updated February 12, 2026 · Developer: Legend Made (com.legend-made.FlowKeep)

Summary: FlowKeep does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data. Your information stays on your device and, if you choose, in your own iCloud account. There are no third-party analytics, no tracking SDKs, and no user accounts to create.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. FlowKeep does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, or any other personally identifiable information. There is no account creation or login process.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

FlowKeep stores the following data locally on your device to provide app functionality:

DataPurpose
Project titles, descriptions, icons, and colorsDisplaying and organizing your projects
Project notes and linksContext preservation
Touch events (timestamps)Freshness, streaks, and queue priority
User preferencesPersonalizing your experience
Notification settingsDelivering reminders you've opted into
Project templatesBuilt-in and user-created templates
Project dependenciesPrerequisites between projects

All of this data is stored in a local database on your device using Apple's Core Data framework within the app's sandboxed container.

3. iCloud Sync

If you are signed into iCloud, FlowKeep can sync your data across your Apple devices using Apple's CloudKit service. Sync is handled by Apple's infrastructure; data is encrypted in transit and at rest. FlowKeep does not operate any servers — your data goes from your device to your iCloud account only.

See Apple's iCloud Privacy Overview.

4. Notifications

FlowKeep may request permission to send local notifications (freshness reminders, queue alerts). Notifications are optional and no notification data is sent to any external service.

5. Widgets & Siri Shortcuts

Home Screen widgets, Lock Screen widgets, and Siri Shortcuts read project data from a shared on-device App Group container. No data is transmitted externally.

6. Template Sharing

When you share a project template via QR code or link:

  • Template data (title, description, structure) is encoded into the QR code or URL.
  • No data is uploaded to FlowKeep servers (we don't operate any).
  • Shared template links may be hosted on flowkeep.github.io as static GitHub Pages content. These pages do not collect user data or analytics.

7. Accountability Buddy (Not Yet Fully Implemented)

The Accountability Buddy UI exists, but no data is exchanged between users today. Invite codes are generated locally and do not connect to any server. When fully implemented, only project titles would be shared with a connected buddy; notes and other details would remain private. This policy will be updated before the feature goes live.

8. AI Features (Disabled)

Optional AI features are currently disabled. No data is sent to Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, or any other AI provider. This policy will be updated before any AI features are enabled.

9. Analytics & Tracking

FlowKeep does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services. No advertising identifiers are accessed. A minimal local event log (last 100 events) is kept on-device for diagnostics only — it is never transmitted.

10. Third-Party SDKs

FlowKeep uses zero third-party SDKs. The app is built with native Apple frameworks: SwiftUI, Core Data, CloudKit, WidgetKit, UserNotifications, App Intents, Foundation, and OSLog.

11. Data Retention & Deletion

Delete the FlowKeep app to remove local data. Manage iCloud storage via iOS Settings if sync was enabled. FlowKeep does not retain data on external servers.

12. Children's Privacy

FlowKeep does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 and is compliant with COPPA.

13. App Store Privacy Label

Based on current implementation: Data Not Collected; Data Not Linked to You; Data Not Used to Track You.

14. Apple Privacy Manifest

FlowKeep includes PrivacyInfo.xcprivacy declaring required API usage (UserDefaults, file timestamps, system boot time, disk space) for app functionality only. NSPrivacyTracking is false.

15. Changes to This Policy

We will update this page and the effective date when practices change, especially before Accountability Buddy or AI features launch.

16. Contact

Questions about this policy or FlowKeep's data practices: support@flowkeep.app
